FAQ

Meal: What if I can't find the right ingredient?

A: You can register your own ingredients for your meals. Simply click the button next to the search bar and enter your ingredient's nutritional values.

Activity: What does intensity mean?

A: The intensity options can be understood using their multipliers. Burning (1x multiplier) represents an average exercise, Smouldering (0.5x) is for a more relaxed session, and Inferno and Hellfire (1.5x and 2x) are for more aggressive workouts.

Activity: Can I add multiple exercises to an activity?

A: No, as every activity consists of 1 exercise and it's duration/intensity. If you've done more than one exercise in your session, they must be registered individually.

Groups: Can I leave a group I created?

A: Yes, you can click the X button next to your name, which you can find on the right side of your group page

Groups: How do I invite people to a group?

A: If you're a group admin, you can invite people by pressing the + button next to the members section of the group page.

Profile: Can I change my username?

A: No, unfortunately you cannot change your account username.
